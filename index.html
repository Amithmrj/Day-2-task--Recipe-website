Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (optional, for extra richness)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes until lightly browned.
  • Mix in yogurt (if using) and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Add water, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  • Stir in garam masala and cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
